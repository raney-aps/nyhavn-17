Private Dining at Nyhavn 17

Nyhavn 17 is the oldest tavern in Nyhavn — proudly serving guests since 1936. In our historic waterfront setting, we create memorable experiences for both intimate gatherings and larger parties.

Birthdays · Confirmations · Weddings · Corporate

Head Chef Christian

Experience from Geranium, d'Angleterre & Studio

Head chef and owner Christian leads the kitchen, bringing experience from renowned Michelin restaurants such as Geranium, d'Angleterre, and Studio. This ensures a high level of gastronomy — whether you prefer something simple or truly exclusive.

We offer tailored group menus in close collaboration with our head chef, combining your wishes, seasonal ingredients, and our culinary expertise.

Private events available for groups of 12 guests or more

Our Kitchen & Style

A proud English/Danish gastropub

On a daily basis, we are a proud English/Danish gastropub, celebrating simple, well-executed dishes. Our menu features classics such as crispy fish'n'chips, traditional Danish "stjerneskud," a juicy 230g burger, and our well-known giant schnitzel — all prepared with respect for quality ingredients and craftsmanship.

This philosophy carries through to our private dining — where the classic and the refined meet.
