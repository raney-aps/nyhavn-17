What to Expect

A Night on the Canal

From 21:30 to 00:30, our historic tavern comes alive with talented musicians, creating an international and energetic ambiance right by the waterfront. Expect everything from classic sing-alongs to lively performances that bring people together across cultures.

Enjoy great food and drinks while the music plays — whether you're here for dinner, a late-night gathering, or just the atmosphere, live music is always part of the experience.
