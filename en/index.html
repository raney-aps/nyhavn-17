Our Story

Four Generations,
One Address

What started as a sailor's tavern in 1936 has grown into one of Copenhagen's most cherished places to eat and drink. The canal outside has changed. The city has changed. But the warmth inside Nyhavn 17 has not.

Today, father and son carry the tradition into its fourth generation — European craft with a very Danish soul.
