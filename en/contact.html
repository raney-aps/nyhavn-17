Getting Here

By Metro

Take the M1 or M2 to Kongens Nytorv station. Nyhavn is a 3-minute walk from the exit. Follow the signs to the canal — you'll find us at number 17.

By Bicycle

Copenhagen is made for cycling. Bike parking is available along the canal at Nyhavn. The address is directly on the waterfront, easy to spot by the colourful buildings.

By Car

Nyhavn is in the heart of central Copenhagen. Paid parking is available nearby at Kvæsthusbroen and Kongens Nytorv. We recommend public transport or cycling during summer.

On Foot

A 10-minute walk from Strøget, 5 minutes from Kongens Nytorv. Walk toward the canal, turn right, and we're the one with the warm light in the window.
