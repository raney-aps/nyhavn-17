Music on the Canal

Live Events

"Good food, good drinks, and the sound of live music drifting out over the water."

Every weekend, we bring live music to our bar — jazz, acoustic, soul, and everything that fits a candlelit evening on Nyhavn. No cover charge, no reservations required for the bar. Just show up, grab a seat, and let the night unfold.
